Blackened Salmon

Description

Using Butter and spices we can achieve restaurant level blackened salmon at home.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Blackening Spices
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Pat the salmon fillets dry, melt the butter, and stir the blackening rub ingredients together.
  2. Brush fillets with butter and rub spices onto them.
  3. Start the fllets flesh side down (Spice-Side), cook for a few minutes then flip.
  4. Top them with lemon juice.
